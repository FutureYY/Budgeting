Saving

Saving money allows you to create a safety net for your future expenses as well as unplanned financial needs.

How do we save money?

Key ideas for saving money

Recording your expenses

Keeping track of all expenses allows you to figure out what you spend on, giving you a clear view on where your money is going.
From here, you will be able to cut back on non-essentials, like entertainment or dining out, to allow you to save more.

Take advantage of student discounts

Many places provide student discounts so bring along your student ID to enjoy those discounts!

Do not buy items impulsively!

It is easy to succumb to urges to buy certain items maybe because it is trending, but is it really a need?
Take time to consider before purchasing and determine whether a certain item is worth its price or if it is neededed.
