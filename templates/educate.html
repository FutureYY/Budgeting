What Is Financial Literacy?

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and effectively use various financial skills.
(including personal financial management, budgeting, and investing)

Benefits of Financial Literacy

Enables you to make well-informed financial decisions
Financial Independence
Help to manage your money effectively
Boost your ability to accumulate wealth over time
Prepares you for any financial emergencies
Helps you to reach your goals, like buying your own laptop etc.
